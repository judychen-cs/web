Main Types of Balloon Creations

Wearable Balloons

Wearable balloon art has been an attempt at recreating vintage and/or peculiar fashion. One prime classification of this are the balloon dresses, which are mentioned in the FAQ section (check it out for more info on balloon dresses specifically). At this point, wearable balloon art has become an industry that supplies to birthday parties, celebrity supermodels, etc.

Non-Wearables

The most common form of non-wearables are the animals, which take the form of dogs, sloths, and all kinds of mammals. More information on this is displayed under the first artists in the famous artists tab.
