What Exactly is Deoxyribonucleic Acid?

Deoxyribonucleic acid, more commonly known as DNA, is what makes you and I different, yet more similar that we could ever expect.

What exactly do I mean, you may ask?

Well, humans share 99.9% of their DNA with each other, and 44.1% with bananas. No, your sibling is not a banana, but all life on Earth can be traced back to common ancestors in phylogenetic trees.

Phylogenetic trees help with determining evolutionary relatedness between species, but not between you and your distant cousin; that is because you two are part of the same species (hopefully).
